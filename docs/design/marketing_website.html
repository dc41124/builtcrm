Frequently asked questions

Homeowner
Do my subs and clients need their own paid accounts?
BuiltCRM
Nope. Subcontractor and client portal access is always free — they're included in your plan. You only pay based on your team size and project count. Everyone else gets in at no extra cost.
General Contractor
We're growing fast. Can I switch plans as we scale?
BuiltCRM
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be prorated for the remainder. Downgrade takes effect at your next billing period.
General Contractor
What counts as an "active project" for the Starter limit?
BuiltCRM
Any project that isn't archived. Once a project is complete and you archive it, it no longer counts against your limit — and you can still access all its records anytime.
Commercial Owner
How secure is our project data? We have NDAs on everything.
BuiltCRM
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use row-level security so each organization's data is fully isolated. Enterprise plans also support SSO/SAML for your IT requirements.
General Contractor
We use QuickBooks. Any integrations coming?
BuiltCRM
QuickBooks, Sage, and Xero integrations are on the roadmap and coming soon. Enterprise plans can access custom integration support today if you need it sooner.
